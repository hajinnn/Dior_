    RÊVE D'INFINI MITZAH SCARF

    $320.00

    The Rêve d'Infini Mitzah scarf in gray multicolor silk twill by Pietro Ruffo offers a dreamlike new take on Christian Dior's favorite star symbol, an essential House code. A timeless style, its elongated shape and pointed ends are embellished with a contrasting 'CHRISTIAN DIOR' signature jacquard band on the back. The Mitzah scarf will lend the finishing touch to any modern outfit and may be tied around the neck, at the wrist or on a bag.

    30 MONTAIGNE NANO POUCH

    $1,450.00

    The 30 Montaigne nano pouch is an original and functional accessory with a signature look. Crafted in blue Dior Oblique jacquard, it features a 'CD' signature element on its flap. A belt may be inserted through its rear loops, and it can also be worn crossbody with its removable chain strap.

    PETIT CD BRACELET

    $530.00

    The Petit CD bracelet has an elegant and streamlined aesthetic. The style is comprised of a thin gold-finish metal chain showcasing a 'CD' signature at the center. A small white resin pearl completes the look. Modern and refined, the bracelet may be worn with other Petit CD creations.
